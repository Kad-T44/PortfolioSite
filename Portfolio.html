About Me

 
            The best word to describe me would undoubtedly be 'creative'. Everything that
        I do or learn is just one step closer towards using those skills to create
        something new, whether that be a new painting, book, sculpture, game, song, etc.
        Creativity is at the heart of all of my curiosities and passions, and always
        has been!
            I am very passionate about music in particular, nd can rant about the history
        importance of certain music genre's for HOURS at a time, if someone let me! Chances
        are you'll definitely learn something new when talking to me about music, like
        the political significance of japanese noise music, or the rise of the many alt
        subcultures in retaliation to oppression. Test me sometime!
